Are we eating the right amount at NID Bangalore?

  1. Main
  2. Making

Context

The modern lifestyle has witnessed a significant shift towards health consciousness, with an increasing number of individuals becoming mindful of their calorie intake and dietary habits. This trend is now noticeable among the students of NID Bangalore, where a growing community of gym-goers and calorie-conscious individuals is emerging. Despite their best efforts, many students struggle with portion control, often due to the large plates and unlimited food options available in the college canteen.

The WHY

At NID Bangalore, it's not uncommon to hear students complaining about weight gain since joining the college. One of the reasons behind this issue is often the difficulty in controlling portions and understanding serving sizes, which is furthered by the canteen's unlimited food offerings. With an abundance of food available, students frequently consume more than their bodies require, leading to unintended weight gain.

Objective

The primary objective of the project is to assist NID Bangalore students in achieving and maintaining a healthy diet by providing tools for portion control and calorie tracking. By giving a better understanding of serving sizes and caloric needs, the project aims to support students in planning the right amount to eat everyday along with informed dietary choices that align with their fitness and health goals.
